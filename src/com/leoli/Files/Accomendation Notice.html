Dormitory Agreement

We, the residents of this dorm room, agree to follow the rules below to maintain a peaceful, clean, and respectful shared living space.

  1. Treat all roommates with kindness, respect, and empathy at all times.
  2. Respect each other’s personal space, privacy, and belongings—never use or move someone else’s items without permission.
  3. Communicate openly and address concerns directly, calmly, and respectfully.
  4. Keep your own area tidy and free of clutter, including bed, desk, and storage.
  5. Participate fairly in cleaning common areas (floor, bathroom, sink, etc.) according to an agreed-upon schedule.
  6. Wash your dishes immediately after use and never leave them in the sink overnight.
  7. Take out the trash when it’s full or when it’s your turn, and replace the bag afterward.
  8. Observe quiet hours from 10:00 PM to 8:00 AM on weekdays and from 12:00 AM to 10:00 AM on weekends.
  9. Use headphones when listening to music, watching videos, or playing games during quiet hours or when others are sleeping.
  10. Avoid loud talking, phone calls, or noisy activities late at night or early in the morning.
  11. Always inform all roommates in advance before inviting any guest to the room.
  12. Guests may not stay overnight without unanimous agreement from all roommates.
  13. You are fully responsible for your guest’s behavior and any mess they create.
  14. Clean shared appliances (microwave, fridge, kettle, etc.) immediately after using them.
  15. Do not hoard food or personal items in common spaces like the fridge or shelves.
  16. Contribute fairly to shared supplies such as toilet paper, soap, cleaning products, and trash bags.
  17. Never share door codes, keys, or access cards with anyone outside the room.
  18. Lock the door when leaving, even for a short time, to ensure everyone’s safety.
  19. Report maintenance issues (leaks, broken lights, etc.) to housing staff promptly.
  20. No smoking, vaping, drugs, alcohol (if prohibited by policy), or illegal activities in the dorm.
  21. If you break something communal, take responsibility and help replace or repair it.
  22. Be mindful of electricity and water usage—turn off lights, AC, and taps when not needed.
By signing below, we pledge to uphold this agreement and support one another as considerate roommates.
